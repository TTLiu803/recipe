SWEET AND SOUR CHICKEN BREAST

Ingredient

Chicken Breast         Two Piece

Starch         Ten Teaspoons

White peppr         15g

Salt         20g

Soy Sauce         Three Teaspoons

Sugar         30g

Garlic         Four Slice

Cut chicken breast into pieces,
add 1 teaspoon salt,
1 / 2 tsp white pepper,
1 / 2 teaspoon soy sauce,
1 / 2 teaspoon sugar
and garlic,
mix well, marinate for more than 30 minutes

Mix the sweet and sour juice in a large bowl.
Mix 2 tsp starch and 10 TSP water,
add 2 tsp tomato sauce, 2 tsp soy sauce, 4 tsp vinegar, 6 tsp tsp sugar,
mix well.

Add the egg liquid of two eggs into the salted chicken breast,
10 teaspoons starch and stir evenly.
Heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
After the oil temperature rises, put the chicken breast one by one.
Keep the fire medium and remove the meat as soon as it floats.
Deep fry again.
Heat the oil until it starts to smoke.
Pour in all the chicken and fry for 30 seconds
Stir fry the juice.
Pour the mixed juice into the frying pan,
heat it over low heat and stir it continuously.
It will thicken gradually with the heating.

Pour in the chicken after solidification and stir well.
